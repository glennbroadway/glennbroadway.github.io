The Grand Chamber

You sit down on the fancy throne and think to yourself "This feels VERY familiar". Just then a bell rings and three servants rush in through a tiny door that you hadn't noticed. They approach you with cape and a crown in their outstretched hands.

"Your majesty! We've been looking everywhere for you." says the first servant, "Hurry up and put on your Crown and Cape - it's almost time for the ceremony..."

It looks like you must have bumped your head in that hallway and become a bit confused! All fine now though, and HEY! YOU'RE THE KING!

THE END
